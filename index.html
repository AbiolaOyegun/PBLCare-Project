EchoCare Evolution Complaint Management System: AKT21 Project with PBL Care Limited

This repository contains all the documentation and presentation materials related to the EchoCare Evolution Complaint Management System.

Introduction

This project aims to enhance the efficiency and effectiveness of complaint management in PBL Care through advanced technological solutions. The following documents and presentations provide detailed insights into the project's objectives, methodologies, progress, and outcomes.

In the ever-evolving landscape of home care services, maintaining standards and swift responses to complaints are crucial. PBL Care Limited, a leader in the sector, has always been committed to excellence. This commitment is evident in their multiple awards and high ratings from the Care Quality Commission (CQC). In collaboration with Birmingham City University (BCU), known for its academic expertise in Computer Science and Artificial intelligence, PBL Care engages in the AKT2I project. This project aimed to develop the EchoCare Evolution Complaint Management System, transforming their complaint handling process into an automated process.
